Overview

Purpose

Our Purpose is to teach and guide you as you learn more about the river ecology to have a safe and enjoyable trip.

Audience

Are you an experienced rafter, or just getting started? We can provoide a tailored experience to you! Are you wanting to learn more about the rivers you raft, and its ecology? We have experts who can teach you all about that! Are you looking for a new challenge to sharpen your skills and abilities? We have the equipment to help, as well as team members to recommend specific rivers for you to raft!
