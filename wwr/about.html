White Water Rafting LLC

At White Water Rafting, our purpose is to ignite the spirit of adventure and foster a deep connection with nature through exhilarating rafting experiences. We are dedicated to providing safe, thrilling, and unforgettable journeys down some of the world's most stunning rivers. We strive to create transformative moments that inspire awe, promote outdoor exploration, and leave a lasting impact on our guests. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-time adventurer, we invite you to join us on a voyage of discovery, where every paddle stroke is a step towards uncovering the beauty and wonder of the great outdoors.

History

White Water Rafting, established in 2000 by Dustin and Rachel Davis, began as a small family-owned operation. They wanted to share their love for rafting with others. Starting with just a few rafts and a small basecamp, they embarked on a journey to provide unforgettable experiences for thrill-seekers of all ages.

Today, White Water Rafting remains committed to its founding principles, continuing to foster a sense of wonder and excitement in every guest who embarks on a journey with them, ensuring that the spirit of adventure lives on for generations to come.
